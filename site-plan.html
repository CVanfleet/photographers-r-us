Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to allow couples, families, graduates, or anyone else to find a photographer near them to take their family picture, graduation pictures or pictures for any occasion! It will also allow photographers to get their name out and get more clients.

Audience

The audience will be directed to families who are finally taking those family pictures that they have been procrastinating for so long, or the graduates who want to remember their anticipated accomplishments. Or anyone wanting to get their picture taken!

For this to work, we need photographers! This will also be geared towards profession or casual photographers that so they can share their work and get more clients.

Typography

Heading Font: Barlow Semi Condensed

With this website, I am going for 'Clean' and 'Professional' and more of a modern look. That is what we get with Barlow Semi Condensed.

Paragraph Font: Overlock

Overlock seemed to fit nicely with how the headings looks as Barlow Semi Condensed. It also pulls off the professional but 'friendly' look.
